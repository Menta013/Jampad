Description: Jampad is a compact 3×3 mechanical macropad powered by a
through-hole Seeed Studio XIAO RP2040. It has a rotary volume encoder, a
0.91-inch OLED, an addressable RGB status LED, and a custom two-piece
3D-printed enclosure. Its default layer provides editing shortcuts, media
controls, Escape, Windows screenshot, and Delete.
